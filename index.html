FAQ
What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a competition-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development divided into small teams to develop a product.

What to bring?

Photo ID; laptop, cell phone, chargers, sleeping bag, toiletries.

Do I need to have an idea or team?

No, you donâ€™t. In fact, we would prefer if you come with a (metaphorical) blank piece of paper to ensure that everyone has the same starting point. You can come with predetermined teams but there are plenty of smart individuals around you with whom you can form new teams.

Who can attend?

Anyone with a sound understanding and some practical experience in computer science.

Maximum number of people per team

We recommend working in teams of 3-4 people. You can work in smaller teams (and even solo if you prefer) but we cap the maximum number of members per team to 5.
